Services

LMES offers a wide range of services which have been utilised in major telecommunication and power projects throughout Ireland and the UK for contractors and industrial clients.

The scope of work and equipment covers all types of cable installations from blown fibre projects to HV electric cable installations. We have an excellent reputation within the industry for our modern and well maintained equipment, our high standard of workmanship and our completion of works on time, safely and professionally.

Cable Pulling And Installation
  • Cable - 10kv to 33kv
  • Cable - 110kv to 220kv
  • Fibre

All cable pulling is carried out in line with Eirgird and ESB Networks specifications. Full data capture of forces, speed and distance of each pull is recorded and provided.

Duct Testing & Proving

Testing of existing or newly installed ducting by mandrel, brush and sponge proving and cleaning to Eirgird and ESB Networks specifications. Data recorded during this process is force, speed and distance for quality records.

Duct Trenching & Civil Works

The installation of LV to HV ducting to Eirgird and ESB Networks Specifications including communications ducting

Winch Calibration

All winch and drum carrier plant used are owned by LMES and calibrated yearly. Calibration certs are issued before each job is started.

Winch Hire

LMES also hires out their plant to other experienced cable pulling contractors if required.
